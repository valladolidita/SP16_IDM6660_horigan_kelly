Festival Internacional is hosted by the Asociación de Cónyuges de los Diplomáticos Acreditados en Colombia.

Started in 2010, the festival is a cultural celebration of live music, art, dance, and authentic food from around the world.

Join us for a day of gastronomía, cultura, artesanías, and fun!

  • $25.000 | 10AM – 4PM | May 22nd
  • Liceo de Cervantes El Retiro
  • Cra. 8 #83 – 29 | Bogotá, Colombia
