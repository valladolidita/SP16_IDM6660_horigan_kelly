Fun Facts About Colombia

With mountains, jungles, beaches, coffee farms, modern cities, and more, Colombia has it all. Here are 2 facts you may not know about this South American gem!

1. Vamos a la playa

Colombia is the only South American country with Atlantic and Pacific coastlines. With over 300 beaches in the country, you can have your pick.

2. Birds, birds, and more birds

Home to 1,879 species of bird, Colombia has most diversity of birds in the world.
